Events and news

“New leash on life: 12 Dogs adopted in June!”

We’re thrilled to announce that 12 of our rescued pups found their forever homes this month. From shy seniors to bouncy puppies, each adoption is a step closer to our mission. Thank you to all the amazing families who opened their hearts and homes.

“Pawpalooza 2025: Community Event raises $5000+”

Our annual Pawpalooza was a tail wagging success! With local vendors, dog games and live music, we raised over $5000 to support vet care and supplies for our rescues. Huge thanks to our volunteers, donors, and the amazing turnout from the community.
