OUR AMAZING LADY LEADS

CEO- Maya Thompson

Maya Thompson brings over a decade of animal welfare leadership to Pawsprint Rescue. Her passion for giving every dog a second chance fuels the organization’s mission and vison. Under her guidance, Pawsprint has grown into a trusted name in the rescue community. Maya believes every tail deserves to wag with hope- and she works tirelessly to make it happen.

CFO- Rachel Singh

With a background in Non Profit Finance and a soft spot for senior dogs, Rachel Singh ensures every donation is maximized to support medical care, food, and shelter. Her financial strategies keep Pawsprint thriving while staying true to its grass roots mission. She’s the reason we can say, ”Every penny has a pa print”.

COO- Danielle Kim

Danielle Kim oversees daily operations with a heart full of compassion and a schedule full of dog walks. From intake to adoption follow-ups, she makes sure the team runs like a oil-wheeled leash. Her calm leadership and hands-on approach help every rescue feel safe, cared for and loved on the path to their forever homes.

Stakeholder- Olivia M

Linda has been with Pawprints Rescue since day one, offering comfort and consistency to every dog that comes through our doors. Her calm, nurturing energy helps scared or anxious pups feel safe, seen, and loved. Marcus brings a quiet strength to our rescue family. Known for his early morning walks and weekend play sessions, he’s helped countless dogs learn to trust again. How we Operate?
How we Operate?
Process

Every rescue journey starts with a call for help. We assess each dog’s needs and bring them into a safe, welcoming environment. From intake and medical checks to training and adoption matching, each step is handled with care.

BTS

Our team is small but mighty — volunteers, fosters, and dog lovers working around the clock. From early-morning feedings to late-night vet runs, we handle each moment with dedication.

Health

Each dog receives full medical care: checkups, vaccinations, and treatments. We also provide behavior evaluations and training so every pup is adoption-ready.

Funds

Transparency matters. Every donation supports vet bills, food, grooming, training supplies, transportation, and adoption outreach programs.

MORE ABOUT OUR SHELTER

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need, ensuring every paw has a chance at love and safety. We work tirelessly to create a future where no animal is left behind.

Our vision is a world where compassion guides every action, and every animal is valued. Through education, outreach, and advocacy, we strive for a future of kindness and care.

From humble beginnings to countless tail wags, our story is built on hope, dedication, and community. Each rescue, each home, each happy ending — it’s all part of our journey.
